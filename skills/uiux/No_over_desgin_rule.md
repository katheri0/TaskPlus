## Preservation Rules

Before proposing changes, identify:

- Core visual identity
- Brand colors
- Typography style
- Component style
- Layout style
- Navigation model
- Interaction model

These elements should remain unless there is a clear usability, accessibility, or business reason to modify them.

Do NOT:
- Change the color palette unnecessarily.
- Replace icons without justification.
- Change navigation patterns without evidence.
- Rebuild layouts solely for aesthetic reasons.
- Introduce trendy effects that reduce clarity.

## Change Classification

Every recommendation must be labeled as one of:

- Critical
  - Fixes accessibility or severe usability problems.

- High Impact
  - Significantly improves usability or comprehension.

- Medium Impact
  - Improves clarity or consistency.

- Low Impact
  - Cosmetic improvement only.

- Optional
  - Personal preference or stylistic enhancement.
